Caritas Community Hospital is a private , non-profit making hospital hospital which strive to provide access to quality healthcare to all who need it. For over four years , Caritas Community Hospital has been taking care of patients all over Kenya. The hospital has a bed capacity of 50 beds and is registered under medical practitioners and dentists act.

To be a leading Hospital known for meeting the health care needs of the entire community through in-comparable patients care and at an affordable costs .
